PRESIDENT BOMBER
The Red Team wins if the President and Bomber are in the same room at the end of the game[cite: 3]. The Blue Team wins if the President and Bomber are in different rooms at the end of the game[cite: 4].
ANGEL ANGEL
You must always verbally tell the truth. This means that you are permitted to lie as long as it is not verbally[cite: 6].
BLIND BLIND
You cannot open your eyes during the game. Don't worry, it is a short game[cite: 9].
CLOWN CLOWN
Do your best to smile at all times[cite: 11].
DEMON DEMON
You must always verbally tell lies. This means that you are permitted to tell the truth as long as it is not verbally[cite: 15, 16].
MIME MIME
You cannot talk. Do your best to not make any noise[cite: 18].
SHY GUY SHY GUY
You cannot show other players any part of your card. You are allowed to see other peoples cards[cite: 22].
SPY SPY
This is a special character card that is the color of the opposite team. This means that the red Spy has an allegiance to the Red Team, but their card is blue[cite: 24, 25].
USURPER USURPER
You have the USURPER power: during any round but the last, you may publicly reveal your card and become the leader[cite: 28]. However, your card must permanently remain publicly revealed for the rest of the game[cite: 29]. This means that you can only use this power once[cite: 30]. You cannot be usurped during the same round in which you used your USURPER power[cite: 31].
AGORAPHOBE
You win as long as you never leave your initial room[cite: 39].
GAMBLER
At the end of the last round, before all players reveal their cards, you must publicly announce which team you think won the game. Win only if you are correct[cite: 41, 42].
HOT POTATO
Any player that card shares with you immediately trades cards with you[cite: 44]. If you colour share, you don't swap cards[cite: 45]. Whoever has the Hot Potato loses at the end of the game[cite: 46].
M16
You win if you card share with the Bomber and the President before the end of the game[cite: 54].
JULIET ROMEO
You win if you are in the same room as each other and the Bomber at the end of the game[cite: 52].
SURVIVOR RIVAL
Survivor wins if they are NOT in the same room as the Bomber at the end of the game[cite: 57]. Rival wins if they are NOT in the same room as the President at the end of the game[cite: 58].
END OF A ROUND
1. LEADER SELECTS HOSTAGES: Room needs to know who is picked. You can't pick yourself.
2. LEADERS MEET: Between the two rooms without hostages.
3. EXCHANGE HOSTAGES: Leaders call out their hostages, and they swap rooms.
4. START TIMER & RESUME: Leaders & hostages return to their rooms, and the timer is started.
